We are introducing SABATH, a dedicated hub for scientific AI benchmarks and tools. SABATH aims to provide a centralized location for the community to access and share tools that facilitate the benchmarking of AI models in scientific applications.

By integrating various tools and benchmarks, SABATH helps researchers streamline the process of evaluating AI performance across different scientific domains, ensuring that the tools used are accessible and reproducible.

The project is available on GitHub: [https://github.com/icl-utk-edu/sabath](https://github.com/icl-utk-edu/sabath)
